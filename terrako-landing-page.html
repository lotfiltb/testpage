TERRAKO: YOUR 24/7 ONLINE SHOP

Highlighting the key milestones that have shaped Terrako's journey. Continually improving, we strive to provide the best possible user experience with each new development. These milestones represent our ongoing efforts to innovate and serve our customers better.

Timeline with Centered Circles and Hover Effect

Milestone 1: Project initiation

December 2019

Kubota Group decides to create an online Marketplace.

Milestone 2: Project definition and creation

From 2020 to 2023

Over the years, the Group collaborated with internal teams and external experts to carefully design and build the platform to meet our business needs.

Milestone 3: Parts Pilot phase

From September 2023 to May 2024

Terrako Parts B2B launch began in September 2023 with 6 pilot UK dealers, followed a few months later by the French phase with 8 pilot dealers.

Milestone 4: B2B Full Launch

From May to September 2024

In France, Terrako ordering has been extended to the entire network as part of a phased onboarding for French dealers for B2B.
